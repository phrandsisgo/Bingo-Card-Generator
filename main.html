Sziget Challenge Bingo: The Official Rulebook The Goal: Complete a full line of challenges – horizontally, vertically, or diagonally – to achieve BINGO! How to Play: Complete a Challenge: To mark a square, you must successfully complete the challenge described. Proof is Key: For verification, take a photo or video of yourself completing the challenge. For some, a witness (another Szitizen or friend) can also confirm. This proof is crucial if you win! Mark Your Square: Once a challenge is completed, mark the square on your card with a pen, marker, or even a sticker. Shout "BINGO!": When you complete five challenges in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal), yell "BINGO!" loudly and proudly! Claim Your Prize (for the first two!): Once you've shouted BINGO, send your photo/video proof for the completed line to the contact below. The first two verified winners will receive a small prize package from Switzerland! The prize includes: A delicious Swiss chocolate bar, a handwritten congratulation letter, and some other small Sziget-themed surprises (total value including shipping max. 20 CHF). One Card, One Player: Each card is unique to you. Do not swap cards or share completed squares with others. Be Respectful: All challenges should be completed with respect for other festival-goers, staff, and the environment. No illegal activities, please! Have Fun First: This game is about enhancing your Sziget experience, not replacing it. Prioritize enjoying the music, the art, and the community.
