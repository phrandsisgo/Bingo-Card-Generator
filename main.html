Sziget Challenge Bingo: The Unofficial Rulebook! The Goal: Complete the full bingo card. Except the joker. Rules:
  1. Complete a challenge: Once a challenge/task is completed you can mark the square
  2. Shout "BINGO" once you completed the full card (except joker)
  3. One Card, One Player: Each card is unique to you. Do not swap cards or share completed squares with others.
  4. Be Respectful: All challenges should be completed with respect for other Szitizens, staff, and the environment. No illegal activities, please!
  5. Have Fun First: This game is about enhancing your Sziget experience, not replacing it. Prioritize enjoying the music, the art, and the community.
  6. Claim Your Prize. I'm gonna donate 2 prices to the best submissions.
